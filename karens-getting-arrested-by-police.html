Frequently Asked Questions

What is the origin of the name Karen?

The name "Karen" has its origins in Greek, meaning "pure" or "clear." It became popular in English-speaking countries in the mid-20th century.

What does "Karen" mean in slang?

In slang, "Karen" refers to a stereotype of a middle-aged, entitled, and often rude or demanding woman, typically associated with privilege and a tendency to complain or make unreasonable requests.

What is Karen short for?

"Karen" is not typically short for any specific name; it has become a cultural meme rather than an abbreviation.
